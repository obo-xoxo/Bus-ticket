Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bus ticket service.

You can purchase a bus ticket by creating an account on our website, selecting your preferred subscription plan (monthly or annual), and completing the payment through any of our supported payment methods. Once payment is confirmed, your digital ticket will be available in your account.

Your digital ticket comes with a unique QR code that can be scanned by bus conductors. Simply show the QR code from your account when boarding the bus. The QR code is valid for the duration of your subscription period.

Yes, our subscription plans provide access to all bus routes operated by Addis Ababa City Bus Service. You can travel on any route within the city without additional charges during your subscription period.

If you lose your phone or cannot access your digital ticket, you can log in to your account from any device to access your ticket. Alternatively, you can visit any of our customer service centers with your ID for assistance in retrieving your ticket information.

Refund policies vary depending on your subscription type. Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once activated. Annual subscriptions may be eligible for a partial refund within the first 30 days of purchase. Please contact our customer support team for specific details regarding your situation.
